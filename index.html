About me

Hello, myself Mazhar Hussain, Currently a doctoral student. Working on the battery thermal management sytems, overlooking the design and development in Sustainable Energy Research Lab.

I aim to optimize thermal regulation for advanced energy storage applications. I love to work and understand the battery modeling techniques and life-cycle degradation. Passionate about clean energy and efficient solutions, I strive to contribute to the development of sustainable technologies for a greener future. I strongly believe that we can decarbonize and make our cities breathable and sustainable.
